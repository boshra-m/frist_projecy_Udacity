When we think of giant pandas, we think of lovely black and white bears. They are cute and have many fans all over the world.

    The following are 4 fascinating fact:
  • giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear: An adult panda weight more than 45 kilos (100 pounds), and can be about 1.5 meters (5 feet) long!
  • go from pink to white and black (or brown):Panda is born pink, and blind. The iconic black and white color come later, after about three weeks.
  • Pandas are "lazy" They spend as long as 14 hours eating per day, and spend the rest of their time in sleeping.
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth.but they eat bamboo and fruit. We all know them as eating only bamboo, but they like some fruit too, and can eat meat.

Pandas are usually born in August, because the panda's mating months are March to May and gestation is 3 to 5 months...
